Invite a New Team Member

Send an official invitation to create their MediAssist Pro account.

Send Onboarding Invite

The staff member will receive an email with their secure account creation link.

How this works

  • An invite email is sent via your Supabase project's SMTP configuration.
  • The staff member clicks the link, sets their password, and is immediately added to the Auth directory.
  • Once registered, they appear in the Staff Directory above where you can manage their access.

Revoking Access

To immediately disable a staff member's access, go to Supabase Dashboard → Authentication → Users, find their account, and click Ban User. Their session will be invalidated on next request.
